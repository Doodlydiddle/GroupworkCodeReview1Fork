Machine Learning Software translating ancient scripture


Language is itself like a process of natural selection, the process of globalisation and imperialism has spread languages far and wide, leading to a competition of languages. This accelerated the degradation process of less popular languages, leaving a graveyard of dead languages, with no prescenddent to decipher them.

(Tesch, Noah. "Why Do Languages Die?". Encyclopedia Britannica, 19 Feb. 2016, https://www.britannica.com/story/why-do-languages-die. Accessed 27 October 2022.)

The deciphering of these ancient scriptures cannot be understood to be merely academic exercise, they contain within them contemporary information that may aid in:

  • uncovering cultural heritage
  • enlightening dark periods of history
  • reclaiming forgotten knowledge



To aid in the deciphering of these ancient texts MIT's Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory have developed a machine learning algorithm that finds remote equivocations of language to create relationships in semantics between languages to massively accelerate the process of translation
