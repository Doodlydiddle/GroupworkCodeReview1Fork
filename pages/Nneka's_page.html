Adverse impacts of Computer Science on our society

The study and application of computer science has resulted in many positive inovations in our society.However,It has also resulted in quite a few problems.

These include but are not limited to:

  • Automated decision making systems may end up amplifying pre-exiting inequalities in the society.
  • Due to the expensive nature of cutting edge technologies it creates a digital divide between the rich and poor.
  • 'The internet is forever...' The internet's permanent nature means that young people may be haunted by information put on the internet before they were of legal age.
  • The ease at which data can be shared allows for piracy
  • Production,usage and digital waste can potentially have a negative impact on the environment.
  • Technology can be used for survelliance purposes which can infringe on people's privacy and cause safety issues.
  • The internet can also be used as a tool to spread misinformation which causes rifts in the society
  • Big tech companies collect and sell data based on users a major data breach could have major consequences in the society
  • Technology may also have adverse effects on our health
  • Studies show that there is a correlation between Increased use of social media and an increase in psychological and mental illnesses
  • Advancements in nuclear research and military technology increases the chance of nuclear warefare and thus the total wipeout of human life as we know it.
  • Internet scams and other forms of cyber attacks could render certain members of the society vunerable
